06

Clear space

Keep ½ of the mark's height as breathing room on every side. Don't crowd it.
Clear space equals one-half the mark's height (the peak amplitude) on every side. The dashed bound shown is the minimum keep-out; co-mark or place body text outside that line, never inside it. CLEAR = 0.5 × HEIGHT
Background contrast should be at least 3:1 against the mark color. Olive on canvas hits 5.1:1. MIN CONTRAST = 3 : 1
